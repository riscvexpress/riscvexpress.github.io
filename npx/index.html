Neuromorphic Processor eXpress Homepage

Overview

Neuromorphic Processor eXpress (NPX) is an externsible framework
for developing lightweight neuromorphic processors.

From spike-based learning to FPGA prototyping,
NPX streamlines every step by integrating snnTorch, RISC-V eXpress, and Vivado.

Under development by ETRI, South Korea, with support from
the IITP grant (No. RS-2025-02263706), funded by MSIT.
